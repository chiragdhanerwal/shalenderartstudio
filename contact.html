Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer freelancing services?

Yes, we provide freelancing services for individual projects. Whether you need a custom mural, sculpture, or commissioned artwork, we work with clients of all sizes.

Can I visit your office or studio?

Absolutely! Office and studio visits are available by appointment. Please call us to schedule a convenient time to discuss your project in person.

What are your operating hours?

We're available to take calls any day from 10 AM to 9 PM. For studio visits and meetings, please schedule an appointment in advance.

Do you work on large-scale commercial projects?

Yes, we specialize in both residential and commercial projects. We've completed over 100 walls and worked with 50+ clients across different industries.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on size and complexity. Small pieces may take 1-2 weeks, while large murals can take several weeks to months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

Do you provide training services?

Yes, we offer professional art training for individuals and groups. Our experienced artists provide hands-on instruction in various techniques and mediums.
