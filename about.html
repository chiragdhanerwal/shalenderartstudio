About Us

At Shalender Studios, we bring together a team of highly skilled, educated, and experienced professional artists who excel in large-scale wall paintings. Our expert designers and supervisors ensure that every concept is executed with precision, backed by an efficient support staff for on-site management and logistics.

We use premium-quality paints imported from France, the UK, and other countries, ensuring vibrant, long-lasting results. All our artworks are finished with a protective PU coating, making them suitable for both interiors and exteriors.

We can create custom artworks in any size to perfectly complement your surroundings.

Our Specialties Include:

  • Paintings in oil, acrylic, ink, and charcoal pencil, covering Indian, Modern, and European art styles.
  • Trompe L’Oeil Wall Paintings – realistic murals painted directly on walls using imported pigments.
  • Hand-Painted Pots & Lamps (sizes from 10” to 15”).
  • Wall Murals in cement, fiberglass, ceramic, and glass mosaic tiles.
  • Translites & Digital Prints – vibrant graphics for restaurants, fast-food joints, and commercial spaces.
  • Sculptures (animals, humans, cartoons, etc.) in fiberglass, metal, and wood for amusement parks, restaurants, hotels, and other venues.
  • Bulk Paintings for hotels, restaurants, and offices at competitive prices.
  • Signages – creative and durable visual branding solutions.

At Shalender Studios, we transform walls, spaces, and concepts into timeless works of art that inspire and impress.
